# DA Agency Brand Guidelines

A reference for the "Mugen Studio" aesthetic used in the DA Agency website.

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Brand Gradient
Used for high-impact CTAs, badges, and accents.
- **Start:** `#f64e6e` (Vibrant Coral Pink)
- **End:** `#ff795e` (Vibrant Orange)
- **Usage:** `bg-gradient-to-r from-[#f64e6e] to-[#ff795e]`

### Neutral Surface Colors
The foundation of the dark mode aesthetic.
- **Background (Main):** `#050505` (Deep Rich Black) - *Never use #000000*
- **Surface (Cards):** `#0A0A0A` (Card Black)
- **Overlay:** `black/50` with `backdrop-blur-sm`

### Typography Colors
- **Primary Text:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Headings & High Contrast
- **Secondary Text:** `#b8b8b8` (Silver) - Body Copy
- **Tertiary Text:** `#545454` (Dark Grey) - Labels & Muted Elements
- **Input Placeholder:** `#363636`

### Borders & Dividers
- **Subtle:** `white/10` (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`)
- **Hover:** `white/30` (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.3)`)

## 2. Typography
- **Font Family:** `Inter` (Google Fonts)
- **Headings:** `font-weight: 900` (Black), `tracking-tighter`, `uppercase`
- **Labels:** `font-weight: 700` (Bold), `tracking-[0.4em]`, `uppercase`, `text-xs`

## 3. UI Tokens
- **Border Radius:** `rounded-full` (Buttons), `rounded-3xl` (Cards)
- **Shadows:** `shadow-[0_0_40px_-10px_rgba(246,78,110,0.5)]` (Brand Glow)
- **blur:** `backdrop-blur-md` (Standard), `backdrop-blur-xl` (Heavy)
